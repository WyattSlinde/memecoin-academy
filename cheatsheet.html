⚠ Reality check

  • ~1.2% of pump.fun coins ever "graduate". Most go to zero.
  • Bots, snipers & insiders are faster than you. Assume you're slowest at the table.
  • Survival > moonshots. Size small, take profits, don't blow up.

◆ 60-second rug-check

  • Mint authority revoked (no new supply)
  • Liquidity locked / burned
  • Top 10 holders < ~25%
  • Dev holding small (<5%)
  • Not heavily bundled / sniped
  • Volume rising, not fading
  • Real, organic community
  • Liquidity healthy for the mcap (you can exit)

◆ Risk & position size

  • Risk 1–2% of bankroll per trade.
  • Decide take-profit & stop before buying.
  • Take initial back at 2× → play house money.
  • Cut losers fast. Never average down a dying coin.
position = bankroll × risk%
e.g. $1,000 × 2% = $20 max

◆ First trade, in order

  1. Burner wallet + seed on paper (offline)
  2. Real axiom.trade only (bookmark it)
  3. Tiny test deposit
  4. Set slippage, MEV protection, priority fee
  5. Find a coin → run rug-check
  6. Tiny test BUY
  7. Tiny test SELL (prove you can exit!)
  8. Size real entry (1–2%) + write exit plan
  9. Execute plan, then walk away

◆ Trade settings

  • Slippage: max price change you'll accept. Higher for new/volatile, tighter for established.
  • Priority fee: tip to land sooner.
  • Bribe / Jito tip: fastest lane (snipers).
  • MEV protection: ON — avoids sandwiches.

⚠ Scam defense

  • RUG liquidity pulled → check it's locked
  • TRAP honeypot → always test-sell first
  • FAKE phishing clone → bookmark real URL
  • PUMP if someone's begging you to buy, you're the exit
  • Never type your seed into a website. Ever.

◆ On copying "the pros"

  • Copying Cented's trades 10s late = −21.3%.
  • You see winners, never the thousands who blew up (survivorship bias).
  • Steal their principles (staged exits, strict filters, cut losers) — not their clicks.

◆ Lifecycle & Pulse

  • New Pairs → just launched (sec old, max risk)
  • Final Stretch → nearing $69K graduation
  • Migrated → real DEX pool (post-migration dump is common)

◆ Glossary quick-hits

Bonding curve — buys ↑ price, sells ↓ price
Graduation — ~$69K mcap → real DEX
Slippage — price-change tolerance
MEV / sandwich — bots skim your trade
Honeypot — buy but can't sell
Rug — liquidity pulled, → 0
Bundle — insiders buy first block
Exit liquidity — you, buying their top
Moonbag — small piece kept after profits
FOMO — the feeling that buys tops
